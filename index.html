Corona viruses are a large family of respiratory viruses, known to cause illness ranging from the common cold to more severe illnesses such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). The current outbreak has been caused by a strain of corona virus that had not previously detected anywhere in the world before the outbreak was reported in Wuhan, China in December 2019.
